Welcome to the 19th IEEE International Symposium on High-Performance Computer Architecture (HPCA-19)

The International Symposium on High-Performance Computer Architecture provides a high-quality forum for scientists and engineers to present their latest research findings in this rapidly-changing field. HPCA-19 will be held in conjunction with the 18th ACM SIGPLAN Symposium on Principles and Practice of Parallel Programming (PPoPP-2013) and the 11th International Symposium on Code Generation and Optimization (CGO-2013). HPCA-19 will be held in Shenzhen.
